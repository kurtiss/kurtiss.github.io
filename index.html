A Note From Jess & Zac

Our wedding will take place on a family farm, so the walking you do will be on grass, gravel, or a barn floor. There will be a short walk from the parking area to the ceremony and reception, but there are a few closer spaces available if you have concerns about navigating the terrain.

Provisions for Any Weather

We are hoping for a beautiful summer evening as a backdrop for an outdoor ceremony; however, if the weather doesn’t allow for that, we have the option of moving into the barn.

Kids of All Ages

We will have activities available for kiddos of all ages, so please bring the younger folks along. :)

Reception

Our reception will be held directly after the ceremony, at the same location. Rather than a full meal, there will be hors d’oeuvres and desserts. We will also have a fire where you can roast hot dogs and make s’mores.
